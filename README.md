# 🚀 GigSuraksha AI  
### AI-Powered Parametric Income Insurance for Quick Commerce Delivery Workers

---

## 📌 Problem Understanding

India’s quick-commerce delivery partners (Blinkit / Zepto) depend on daily earnings.  
External disruptions such as extreme weather, pollution spikes, or local restrictions reduce their working hours, causing immediate income loss.

### Current Challenges
- No income protection exists  
- Claims systems are slow  
- Workers bear full financial risk  

👉 Our solution provides AI-powered parametric income insurance with automatic payouts.

---

## 👤 Target Persona

### Urban Quick-Commerce Delivery Partner

Example:
- Blinkit rider in Lucknow  
- Works 8–10 hours daily  
- Weekly earnings depend on completed deliveries  
- Highly affected by environmental disruptions  

### Why This Persona?
- Short delivery cycles  
- Hyper-local risk patterns  
- High weather dependency  

---

## ⚡ Solution Overview

GigSuraksha AI provides:

- Weekly subscription-based income insurance  
- AI-based risk pricing  
- Automatic disruption detection  
- Instant payout without manual claim  

---

## 💰 Weekly Pricing Model

Dynamic premium calculated using AI.

### Pricing Factors
- Delivery zone risk score  
- Weather prediction data  
- Historical disruption frequency  
- Worker activity hours  
- Demand density  

Example:
- Low-risk zone → ₹28/week  
- High flood-risk zone → ₹52/week  

Coverage:
- Compensates lost delivery hours  
- Weekly payout cap applied  

---

## 🌧️ Parametric Triggers

- Heavy rainfall (> 60mm/hr)  
- Extreme heat (> 45°C)  
- Hazardous AQI (> 400)  
- Government-imposed local restrictions  
- Demand collapse (> 50% drop in orders)  

---

## 🤖 AI Integration Strategy

### 1️⃣ Risk Prediction Model
Uses:
- Historical weather datasets  
- AQI patterns  
- Urban flood-risk mapping  
- Delivery demand trends  

### 2️⃣ Dynamic Premium Engine
AI adjusts weekly premium per worker.

### 3️⃣ Fraud Detection System
Detects:
- GPS spoofing  
- Fake inactivity  
- Duplicate claims  
- Behaviour anomalies  

Methods:
- Location clustering  
- Time-series anomaly detection  
- Pattern-based risk scoring  

---

## 🔁 User Workflow

1. Worker registers  
2. AI generates risk profile  
3. Weekly premium displayed  
4. Worker activates coverage  
5. System monitors disruptions  
6. Trigger event detected  
7. Claim auto-generated  
8. Fraud AI verification  
9. Instant payout via UPI simulation
